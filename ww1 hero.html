Canadian War Heros

Sir Arthur Currie

He became a hero because he was the first Canadian commander of the Canadian Corps during the First World War. He commanded a regiment at the outbreak of the war in 1914. Later on he became the 1st Canadian Division in September 1915 for the next 2 years.

Filip Konowal, VC

He became recognized for his effort when he fought with bravery at the battle for Hill 70, which he would earn the Victoria Cross for his actions from 22-24 august in 1917. He saved many lives when he was fighting during the war and hes a very important figure in Canadian history.
